| Evaluation design changes the scientific interpretation of structural priors. | supported | `paper/tbl_conclusion_shift.md`, `paper/b_mbpp224_fair_budget.md` | Put this in the title, abstract, and first results paragraph. |

| Structural-prior effects are quality- and information-access-conditioned. | supported diagnostic audit | `paper/tbl_prior_quality_audit.md`, `paper/tbl_prior_quality_response.md`, `paper/tbl_prior_quality_response.json`, `paper/fig_prior_quality_response.md`, `paper/prior_quality_audit_provenance.md`, `paper/provenance_validation_checklist.md` | `structure_fidelity` is retrospective and reference-code-based; the audit reads the stored raw-result field, reports quality coverage, bin-level net delta, harm rate, outcome-level fidelity separation, and complete threshold sensitivity, and must not be presented as a deployable quality estimator or as a tuned causal proof. |
| Core claims do not depend entirely on accepting the prior-quality audit. | supported boundary | `paper/tbl_conclusion_shift.md`, `paper/b_mbpp224_fair_budget.md`, `paper/tbl_prompt_only_structural_mbpp224_control.md`, `paper/audit_reporting_checklist.md` | If the retrospective audit is set aside, the conclusion shift, fixed code-aware diagnostic effect, non-positive prompt-only boundary, bad-prior stress result, and reporting-checklist artifact remain; only the quality-conditioned interpretation is weakened. |
| Prompt-only structural matching is a fixed boundary control. | supported protocol detail | `paper/prompt_only_structural_matcher.md`, `retrieval/prompt_structural.py`, `paper/provenance_validation_checklist.md` | State query-side access precisely: prompt plus entry point only. Say that the formula and constants are the fixed matcher used for both prompt-only structural controls, that the full rerun is not selected from an alternative-weight sweep, and do not present it as a new prior-construction method. |
| Bad priors remain harmful under reranking. | supported | `paper/fig_bad_prior_delta_types.md`, `paper/bad_prior_failure_breakdown.md` | Write as residual harm, not catastrophic harm. |
| External evidence is qualified rather than broad-transfer evidence. | qualified | `paper/tbl_external_v2.md`, `paper/external_protocol.md` | Do not write broad transfer or modern-benchmark generality claims. |
| Cross-model evidence is backend-sensitive rather than invariant. | qualified | `paper/tbl_cross_model_v2.md`, `paper/tbl_cross_model_v2.json`, `paper/tbl_cross_model_deepseek_two_seed.md`, `paper/tbl_cross_model_starcoder2_two_seed.md`, `paper/tbl_cross_model_safe_gpu_seed2.md`, `paper/discussion_backend_sensitivity.md` | Explicitly say backend-sensitive portability and same-scale qualified support across Qwen/DeepSeek, never invariance. | Backend-level structural-prior claims are replicate-sensitive. | supported boundary | `paper/tbl_backend_replicate_boundary.md`, `paper/tbl_backend_replicate_boundary.json`, `paper/backend_replicate_boundary_notes.md` | Granite and StarCoder2 four-replicate aggregates are positive, but write this as replicate-aware backend-boundary evidence. | HumanEval+ and weak-backend boundary instantiations show how the audit pattern applies outside the main table. | qualified | `paper/tbl_boundary_instantiations.md`, `paper/tbl_external_v2.md`, `paper/tbl_cross_model_v2.md` | Treat as boundary instantiation only; positive, neutral, and negative rows are retained. |
| Controlled prior-quality degradation has a completed DeepSeek pilot. | supported secondary diagnostic | `paper/tbl_controlled_degradation_sweep.md`, `results/degradation_sweep/summary_deepseek_seed1_n50.json`, `artifact/CONTROLLED_DEGRADATION_PROTOCOL.md` | Report as a noisy MBPP+50 secondary diagnostic sweep, not as causal proof or as a replacement for the main prior-quality response audit. |
| Verifier and usefulness deliver stable headline gains. | unsupported | project-wide evidence | Keep out of headline claims. |
| We solve safe structural prior usage. | unsupported | project-wide evidence | Must not appear. |
